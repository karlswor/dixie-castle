Instructions for Ordering Take Out

In order to order take out, you have to call in to the proper store you want to go pick up the food from. After you call in and place your order for the food you want. You then go to the location you called in to at the time you said you would pick up the food. When you show up to the resturaunt, you pay and then you can leave with your food.
